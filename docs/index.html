Intro

Web design and development changed a lot in the last twenty+ years and the rate of change does not seem to going to slow down. From very small and simple it is grown to be a huge field with several specializations. But you can still go a long way by simple means and only dive down into complexity when you need it.

My hope is to help you to get started. Don't let get yourself overwhelmed by the amount there is to learn. There is always a lot you don't know yet, even for me doing this for two decades.
